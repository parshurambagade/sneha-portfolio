About me

As a fresh interior design graduate with strong AutoCAD skills, I bring a fresh perspective, dedication, and a focus on simplicity. I prioritize innovative ideas and genuine connections with clients, believing that the core idea and sincere attitude are crucial in interior design.

With my AutoCAD expertise, I seamlessly translate design concepts into precise technical drawings, ensuring accuracy and detail. I am eager to contribute my fresh perspective, dedication, and passion for interior design to create remarkable spaces. Despite being new to the industry, I am excited to embark on this transformative journey and bring clients' visions to life.
